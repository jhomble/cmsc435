Our Application!

Our team has developed a desktop application capable of expediting the research efforts of Dr. Jeffrey Herrmann. The novelty of our application is in its ability to allow the user to define the knowledge base of the new task without ever writing code. By walking through the step by step knowledge builder, the user will be able to generate all files needed to test their experiment.
